✉️ A Letter for You

From the heart, with gratitude.

Dear Daddy 💙,

Happy Father's Day! 🎉👨🏻‍👧🏻


I've never really outgrown the comfort of being your youngest. No matter how old I get, there's a part of me that still feels that if Papa is here, everything will be alright. ❤️ Even today, while you're spending time with your granddaughter 👶🏻💕, that feeling hasn't changed.


You have always handled the money matters 💰, the bank work 🏦, and all those complicated financial things. Maybe I should know more myself, but the truth is that whenever you're around, I feel like there's nothing to worry about. Your presence has always felt like security 🛡️❤️.


Thank you for teaching me not to trust everyone so easily. I've always been soft-hearted 🤍, and although I didn't always like hearing your warnings, life has shown me many times that you were trying to protect me. Your lessons have saved me from mistakes more than once 🙏🏻.


You have never been shy about scolding me when I deserved it 😅. Sometimes I still roll my eyes when you start 🙄, and sometimes our arguments go far beyond the original topic. We think very differently, and sooner or later you end up saying, "You just argue for the sake of arguing." 😂 Maybe you're a little right.


But even through all those disagreements, I've never doubted that everything you say comes from a place of love ❤️.


One of the greatest gifts you've given me is not just support, but a place to grow 🌱. You didn't build a path for me and tell me to follow it. You gave me something even better—a garage to build my own wings 🛠️🦋. You gave me a safe place to learn, fail, try again, and become myself.


And whenever life throws something terrible our way, you remind us:


"Duster marun taak, ani pudhe vadh." ✨


Wipe it clean and move forward. It's one of the most valuable lessons you've ever taught me. 🙏🏻


We may never agree on everything 🤷🏻‍♀️, and our thoughts may never completely match, but I am proud to be your daughter ❤️. Thank you for your protection 🛡️, your guidance 🧭, your stubbornness 😄, your faith in me 🌟, and for always being there when it matters most.


I love you, Daddy. ❤️🤗


With all my love, respect, and endless gratitude,
Your youngest daughter 👧🏻💕
